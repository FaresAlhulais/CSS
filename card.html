Toque macaque

Actually, there are two main categories of Monkeys around the globe. The first one is Old World monkeys, which exist in the African and Asian continents. The second category is New World monkeys, which can be found in South America.

  • Scientific Name: Cercopithecidae
  • Average Length: 44 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 35 years
  • Habitat: Forests and Mountains

The Toque macaques are native and endemic exclusively to Sri Lanka, where they generally occupy three types of habitat, hence can be divided into three subspecies. These are: the Common toque macaque, occurring in dry habitat; the Pale-fronted toque macaque, inhabiting wet areas; and the Hill-zone toque macaque, found at high elevations.
